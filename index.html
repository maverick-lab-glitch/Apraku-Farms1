Our Composting Process

  1. Start with a base layer of coarse, dry materials (carbon source).
  2. Add fresh, moist food scraps and plant clippings (nitrogen source).
  3. Ensure the pile maintains proper moisture (like a wrung-out sponge).
  4. Turn the pile weekly to ensure aeration and speed decomposition.
Read about our Water Conservation Techniques

We use drip irrigation systems to deliver water directly to the plant roots, reducing evaporation by up to 60% compared to traditional methods.

Glossary of Terms

Cover Crop
A crop planted primarily to manage soil erosion, fertility, or biodiversity, not to be harvested for profit.
No-Till Farming
An agricultural technique for growing crops without disturbing the soil through tillage.
